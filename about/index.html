I’m a computer science student at Kuang Yaming Honors School, Nanjing University, passionate about building the foundations of future intelligence. From computer architecture to embodied AI, I thrive on tackling complex, real-world problems that push the boundaries of what machines—and humans—can achieve.

Beyond technical work, I immerse myself in economics, philosophy, political science, psychology, and literature. I have a deep passion for Buddhist philosophy and Stoicism, and I actively practice meditation to cultivate clarity and resilience.

Music and sports are my constant companions. I enjoy listening to virtually every genre—from rap and jazz to classic Cantonese songs, R&B, rock, and more. Feel free to check out my NetEase Cloud Music account if you’re curious. I’m also a dedicated runner and calisthenics enthusiast.

I admire innovators who reshape the world—not for fame, but because they dare to defy limits and rewrite the rules. I strive to be among them: continuously building, learning, and pushing boundaries. The quote ##You only live once## strongly resonates with me. Though it’s sometimes hard to tell whether we live to die or die to live, I believe life is a rewarding and beautiful journey worth embracing fully.

This site is a record of that journey—my pursuit of knowledge, growth, and meaningful impact.
